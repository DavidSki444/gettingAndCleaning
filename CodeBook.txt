Code Book 
=================

The data set provides, for each activity and each subject, the average of the following variables, which are the measurements on the mean and standard deviation for each measurement from the original "Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Dataset" :

(...-mean(): Mean value
...-std(): Standard deviation)

-- Accelerometer - time domain signals (prefix 't' to denote time) :
1 tBodyAcc-mean()-X : body acceleration - X direction - Mean value
2 tBodyAcc-mean()-Y : body acceleration - Y direction - Mean value
3 tBodyAcc-mean()-Z : body acceleration - Z direction - Mean value
4 tBodyAcc-std()-X : body acceleration - X direction - Standard deviation
5 tBodyAcc-std()-Y : body acceleration - Y direction - Standard deviation
6 tBodyAcc-std()-Z : body acceleration - Z direction - Standard deviation
7 tGravityAcc-mean()-X : gravity acceleration - X direction - Mean value
8 tGravityAcc-mean()-Y : gravity acceleration - Y direction - Mean value
9 tGravityAcc-mean()-Z : gravity acceleration - Z direction - Mean value
10 tGravityAcc-std()-X : gravity acceleration - X direction - Standard deviation
11 tGravityAcc-std()-Y : gravity acceleration - Y direction - Standard deviation
12 tGravityAcc-std()-Z : gravity acceleration - Z direction - Standard deviation
-- Jerk signals :
13 tBodyAccJerk-mean()-X : Jerk signals - body acceleration - X direction - Mean value
14 tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Y : Jerk signals - body acceleration - Y direction - Mean value
15 tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Z : Jerk signals - body acceleration - Z direction - Mean value
16 tBodyAccJerk-std()-X : Jerk signals - body acceleration - X direction - Standard deviation
17 tBodyAccJerk-std()-Y : Jerk signals - body acceleration - Y direction - Standard deviation
18 tBodyAccJerk-std()-Z : Jerk signals - body acceleration - Z direction - Standard deviation
-- Gyroscope 3-axial signals :
19 tBodyGyro-mean()-X : gyroscope signals - X direction - Mean value
20 tBodyGyro-mean()-Y : gyroscope signals - Y direction - Mean value
21 tBodyGyro-mean()-Z : gyroscope signals - Z direction - Mean value
22 tBodyGyro-std()-X : gyroscope signals - X direction - Standard deviation
23 tBodyGyro-std()-Y : gyroscope signals - Y direction - Standard deviation
24 tBodyGyro-std()-Z : gyroscope signals - Z direction - Standard deviation
-- Jerk signals :
25 tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-X : Jerk signals - gyroscope signals - X direction - Mean value

29 tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Y : Jerk signals - gyroscope signals - Y direction - Standard deviation
30 tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Z : Jerk signals - gyroscope signals - Z direction - Standard deviation

33 tGravityAccMag-mean() : Magnitude - gravity acceleration - Mean value

36 tBodyAccJerkMag-std() : Magnitude - Jerk signals - body acceleration - Standard deviation
